American singer-songwriter and musician Myles Kennedy has an estimated net worth of $3 million. He is best known as the rhythm guitarist and lead vocalist of Alter Bridge, a popular rock band.

Myles Richard Bass was born on November 27, 1969 in Boston, Massachusetts,

USA. He is an alumnus of Spokane Falls Community College where he studied music theory. He is currently married to his wife Selena. The couple is currently based in Spokane Washington. He has been suffering from tinnitus.

Kennedy's music career started off with the band Cosmic Dust where he played lead guitarist. The band went to release one studio album. Eventually, he became a part of the band, Citizen Swing, which released two albums before the group disbanded.

In 1996, he founded the The Mayfield Four band, where he served as the lead guitarist and the lead vocalist. The band released two albums which were titled Fall Out, and Second Skin. In 2002, the group disbanded and shortly, Kennedy became a member of Alter Bridge, which released four more albums titled One Day Remains, Blackbird, AB III, and Fortress.
