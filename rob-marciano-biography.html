Who is Rob Marciano?

Rob Marciano is a meteorologist and an American journalist. He is currently employed by ABC News and is presently working as the senior meteorologist and weather correspondent for ABC’s GMA.

He holds the American Meteorological Seal of Approval and is a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist (CBM).

Rob Marciano: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

Rob Marciano was born on 25 June 1968, in Glenville, Connecticut.

He belongs to American nationality and he is of mixed (Italian and German) ethnicity.

His birth name is Robert Mark Marciano. He spent his entire childhood in Connecticut.

Since his childhood, he was very eager to know about weather changes and climate.

As a result, because of his passion, he decided to be a Meteorologist.

From which College or University he graduated?

Rob Marciano has completed his Bachelor’s degree in meteorology from Cornell University.

Rob Marciano: Career

Rob Marciano served as chief meteorologist for KATU-TV and 750 KXL News Radio in Portland, Oregon in May 2003. Before joining CNN, he even worked as the morning and then chief meteorologist for KPLC-TV in Louisiana from 1994 to 1997.

In May 2003, Rob Marciano has joined as the co-host of ‘Early Start’ and ‘Starting point’ on CNN. In November 2012, he bid farewell to this show and joined ‘Entertainment Tonight’. He made his debut in GMA on September 6, 2014.
