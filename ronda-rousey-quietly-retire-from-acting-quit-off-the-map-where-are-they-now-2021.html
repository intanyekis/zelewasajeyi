Did Ronda Rousey Quietly Retire From Acting?

In 2021, Ronda Rousey's life is drastically different in comparison to what it once was. Rousey at one point, was on the top of her game, not only dominating her opponents inside the octagon but forging quite the film career, appearing in franchises like 'The Fast and Furious'.

Suddenly, projects started to subside, and she was taking on TV roles, which as we'll assess later, would also end in tough circumstances.

Ronda Rousey Signed A Huge Three-Film Deal With Lifetime In 2016

At the start, it seemed as though Ronda Rousey was in for a promising career. She decided to leave the UFC for good, branching off into the entertainment world. She got a huge deal according to Deadline alongside Lifetime, for a three-picture deal with the network.

Not only that but Rousey was also well on her way to both fame and success, having appeared in films like 'The Expendables 3', 'Furious 7', 'Mile 22' and 'Charlie's Angels'. Each film was flanked alongside some of Hollywood's biggest stars like Vin Diesel, Mark Wahlberg, Sylvester Stallone, and so many others.

It would set her acting career back, along with another gruesome moment that took place on the set of '9-1-1' on FOX.
