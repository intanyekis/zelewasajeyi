How much does Emma Chamberlain make off her podcast?

Emma Frances Chamberlain is a YouTube personality from the United States.

On April 11, 2019, Chamberlain released the first episode of her podcast, Anything Goes (previously known as Stupid Genius).

The exact amount she makes off her podcast is unknown,  however it is reported that she makes  up to $600,000 salary by promoting brands through her social media accounts.

Emma Chamberlain’s podcasts is a weekly series in which she attempts to have an ongoing conversation about any particular issue/topic, produced by Ramble Official in collaboration with Cadence13 and United Talent agency.

She discusses a variety of topics, including relationships, mental health, fashion, and gut feelings.

It can be found on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and a variety of other podcast platforms.

Anything Goes won the Pop Podcast award at the 47th People’s Choice Awards.
