Diddy Spotted Water Rafting In Wyoming Amid Mounting Legal Trouble

Diddy has been in some deep water lately with mounting legal trouble, so he decided to jump into even deeper waters—whitewater rafting, that is.TMZ captured the mogul in action in Jackson Hole, Wyoming, on Sunday (June 30), hitting the waters with two other unnamed figures and an activity guide. The disgraced star can be seen getting in a workout, paddling down the river without a care in the world.

Elsewhere, Sean Combs recently had a day named in his honor revoked. The outlet reported that on Friday (June 28), the Miami Beach commission voted to revoke and abolish “Sean Diddy Combs Day” in the 305.

The commission cited 2016 surveillance footage showing the businessman abusing and beating Cassie on camera at a hotel. The Miami Herald reported that the final decision to overrule his commemorative day arrived on Wednesday (June 26) with no debate. According to TMZ, Combs initially received the honor in October 2016, months after the events on the surveillance video took place.

The footage also garnered a response from Diddy, who apologized to his ex-girlfriend, Cassie. Combs claimed to be disgusted by his “inexcusable” behavior and said he sought therapy for it. The apology felt empty to some fans because Puffy has consistently declared that he was innocent of any allegations against him since November 2023.

In fact, he released his first statement a month after Cassie’s November bombshell lawsuit, typing in all caps, “ENOUGH IS ENOUGH. FOR THE LAST COUPLE OF WEEKS, I HAVE SAT SILENTLY AND WATCHED PEOPLE TRY TO ASSASSINATE MY CHARACTER [AND] DESTROY MY REPUTATION AND MY LEGACY. SICKENING ACCUSATIONS HAVE BEEN MADE AGAINST ME BY INDIVIDUALS LOOKING FOR A QUICK PAYDAY.

However, in a change of heart, he suddenly deleted his apology and all the rest of his posts from Instagram in June.
