Donald Trumps Ex-wife Ivana Dies at 73: Medical Examiner Declares Ivana Trumps Death to Be Acc

Ivana Trump, a former spouse of former President Donald Trump, passed away from “blunt impact injuries” to the body, the pathologist for New York City announced on Friday.

Ivana Trump, a veteran entrepreneur, passed away on Thursday at age 73 at her New York City residence. She was the mother of Eric Trump, Ivanka Trump, and Donald Jr.

There did not “appear to be any criminality” linked with her passing, the New York Police Department had stated on Thursday. A statement claims that at 12:40 p.m. ET, officers received a 911 call about a “assisted individual” and discovered a “73-year-old female who was unconscious and lifeless.”

The New York Fire Department said that it reacted to a report of a person having a cardiac attack at the home, and the timing and location of that reaction matched the ones the NYPD linked to Trump. The guy was reportedly already dead when the fire department arrived.

Trump died at the scene, according to investigators.

Ivana Trump, who was raised in communist Czechoslovakia, collaborated with Donald Trump on some of his most notable real estate ventures. Following his sensational affair with Marla Maples, who eventually married Donald Trump and gave birth to their daughter Tiffany, the two got divorced in 1992.

Ivana Trump continued to live a jet-setting, international lifestyle after the divorce, getting married twice more before divorcing each time.

On Thursday, her children paid tribute to her business skills, sense of humor, and devotion.

In a tweet, Ivanka Trump expressed that “She lived life to the fullest — never giving up the chance to laugh and dance,” the speaker said, “and was grieved by the demise of (her) mother. I will miss her forever and will keep her memory alive in our hearts always.”
