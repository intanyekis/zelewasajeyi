The lyrics to X.O.'s song Orange Soda suggest a desire for a potent combination of OG Kush and orange soda. OG Kush is a popular strain of cannabis known for its strong and relaxing effects, while orange soda is a carbonated beverage with a sweet and citrusy taste. The fusion of these two contrasting substances may symbolize the juxtaposition of high and low, pleasure and pain, or the desire to experience multiple sensations simultaneously.


Furthermore, the repetition of the same lyrics throughout the song emphasizes the importance of this combination, almost as if it's a mantra or a ritual that one must go through to achieve a certain state of mind. This could also speak to the addictive nature of both substances and the repetition of indulging in them. Overall, the lyrics suggest a craving for a unique and potent experience that combines the physical and mental effects of cannabis and the sensory pleasure of a carbonated drink.
