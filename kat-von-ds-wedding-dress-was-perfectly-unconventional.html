Kat von Ds wedding dress was perfectly unconventional

Kat von D and husband Rafael Reyes (A.K.A. Leafar Seyer of Prayers) got married in February and are expecting their first child, but they held a wedding ceremony this Sunday where they got to play up their complimentary goth styles to a lavish extent.



Of course, Kat von D’s vibrant red dress set the Internet abuzz this weekend. Not only was it a striking color with Victorian ruffles, but horn-like headpieces held her gauzy red veil in place.

In a couple hours I’ll be walking down the aisle with the love of my life @thekatvond ❤️ EN VIDA Y EN MUERTE ?

Kat shared a photo of a giant white cross made of melting candles. The wedding also had a “heaven room” and a a red castle cake. Kat even performed piano during the celebration.
