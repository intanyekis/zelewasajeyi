How Travis Barker's Ex-Wife Shanna Moakler Got In The Way Of A Romance Between Himself And Kim Karda

Highlights

  • Kourtney and Kim Kardashian's feud continues on their new show, with Kourtney accusing Kim of using her wedding as a "business opportunity."
  • The tension between the sisters started on their previous show, where they got physical after Kourtney missed a family photoshoot due to mom duties.
  • Travis Barker, who previously had a crush on Kim, has not publicly commented on the sisters' feud, focusing instead on his family after his wife underwent emergency fetal surgery.

Kourtney Kardashian's relationship with her younger sister, Kim Kardashian has been rocky over the past few years. The two seem to have opposing views when it comes to work.

It started on their E! show, Keeping Up With the Kardashians where they famously got physical with each other after Kourtney missed a family photoshoot Kim had squeezed into her busy schedule. The eldest Kardashian sister explained that she had mom duties to prioritize at that time.

Kim then labeled the Poosh "fraudster" the "least exciting to look at," which sparked outrage among fans who thought the Skims "briber" was over-promoting hustle culture.

The tension recently carried onto their new Hulu show, The Kardashians where Kourtney accused Kim of using her Dolce & Gabbana wedding in Italy as a "business opportunity." Now, fans are wondering what Travis Barker feels about the feud since the Blink-182 drummer previously admitted to having a crush on Kim.

Are Kim And Kourtney Kardashian Still Feuding?

As of the current season 4 of their reality show, Kim and Kourtney are still going at it. In the previous season, the latter distanced herself from the family, saying: "With my family, I'm like, 'Protect my energy.' And like, 'I'm married! Bye everyone!'"

Prior to that, she broke down during a conversation with Kendall Jenner. "I feel like there's an abundance of opportunities," she said of Kim's "copied" Dolce & Gabbana collection. "It's not about business, it's just there's so much and it takes precedence over hurting your sister. It's legit copying my wedding."

Kendall said that she could see "both sides" of the situation after Kourtney said that what Kim did "was more personal. I think that's why it hurts my feelings because it was my actual wedding." Kim's catty response went viral with people calling it a first-world problem.

"I got married in Italy. Am I saying you copied me by getting married in Italy?" she said in a confessional interview. "Who performed at my wedding? Andrea Bocelli. Who performed at Kourtney's wedding? Andrea Bocelli. You stole my f--king wedding country and my wedding performer. Andrea Bocelli is my favorite male singer of all time, but I'm copying her dolce vita lifestyle? OK."

The two are on good terms for now after a serious one-on-one talk. But in a recent episode, Kim admitted that they had a group chat called "Not Kourtney." When asked about the members of the group, Kourtney said in her Instagram comments: "I believe it was just my sisters from the surveys I've been taking."

Did Travis Barker Date Kim Kardashian?

In an episode of The Kardashians, Kris Jenner told Kourtney that the musician moved to Calabasas to be close to her. Fans then reminded the internet that Travis dated Kim in 2007, the year he moved to the neighborhood. He even called the former Mrs. Kanye West "f------ hot" and "eye candy" after saying he had a huge "crush" on her.

In his 2016 memoir, Can I Say: Living Large, Cheating Death, and Drums, Drums, Drums, Travis wrote that during a 2006 trip to Amsterdam with his then-girlfriend, Paris Hilton, he was secretly eyeing Kim who was working as the socialite's closet organizer back then.

"I was having a blast and was happy to let the world know it. I kept on secretly checking out Kim, telling Lil Chris [drum tech], 'I don't care if she's the closet girl, she's hot,'" he wrote in the book. They then started talking after Kim's infamous sex tape.

Travis recalled being a "gentleman" to her despite her reputation at that time. "I'd go visit her and her family at their clothing store," he said of their wholesome affair. "We'd be really sweet around each other, like little kids, and then when we were apart, she'd call me and say, 'I want to see you again.' 'Me too,' I'd say."

However, he eventually got back with his ex-wife Shanna Moakler who "caught wind of what was going on. She poured a drink on Kim at a party thrown by Carmen Electra. I felt terrible," said the punk rocker. Travis then clarified that he and Kim "never touched each other. It just wasn't meant to be."

What Does Travis Barker Think About Kim And Kourtney's Feud?

Travis hasn't made any public statement on Kim and Koutney's feud. But the couple seem to be focused on their own family these days. In September 2023, the drummer postponed his tour as his wife had an emergency fetal surgery. In a lengthy Instagram post, Kourtney announced that everything went well with the operation.

"I will be forever grateful to my incredible doctors for saving our baby's life," she wrote back then. "I am eternally grateful to my husband who rushed to my side from tour to be with me in the hospital and take care of me afterwards, my rock. And to my mom, thank you for holding my hand through this."

She continued: "I don't think anyone who hasn't been through a similar situation can begin to understand that feeling of fear. I have a whole new understanding and respect for the mamas who have had to fight for their babies while pregnant. Praise be to God. Walking out of the hospital with my baby boy in my tummy and safe was the truest blessing."
