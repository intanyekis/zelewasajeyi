After high school, Brady attended the University of Michigan, where he played football for the Wolverines. He was the backup quarterback for most of his college career, but he did start during his senior year.

In the 2000 NFL Draft, the New England Patriots selected Brady in the sixth round. Tom Brady Early Life

Tom Brady was born in San Mateo, California on August 3rd, 1977, to a family of sports enthusiasts. His parents, Galynn and Thomas Brady, had three daughters before him: Nancy, Julie, and Maureen. As a child, he attended San Francisco 49ers games with his family and idolized Joe Montana. He also rooted for the Los Angeles Lakers and Boston Celtics.

During his high school years at Junipero Serra High School, Brady played football, baseball, and basketball. He became the starting quarterback during his junior year and made highlight tapes during his senior year to send to colleges he was interested in attending. College recruiting of athletes was different back in the mid-1990s, and rankings were not as widely used as they are today. Despite being a standout athlete, Brady had to narrow down his choices to Cal, UCLA, USC, Michigan, and Illinois. He also received an offer from the Montreal Expos to play baseball in the 18th round of the 1995 MLB Draft. Ultimately, he chose to play college football for the University of Michigan.

During his first two years in college, Brady was the backup quarterback behind future NFL QB Brian Griese and struggled to get playing time. However, he went on to start every game during the 1998 and 1999 seasons and set new Wolverine records for most pass attempts and completions in a season. Michigan won 20 of 25 games that Brady started, and he had an impressive college career, although he was not an early draft pick. In fact, the New England Patriots selected him with the 199th pick in the sixth round of the 2000 draft.

Tom Brady Career

Tom Brady’s NFL career began in 2000 when he was drafted by the New England Patriots in the sixth round of the NFL Draft. He started as a backup quarterback but quickly earned the starting job after an injury to Drew Bledsoe. In his first season as a starter, Brady led the Patriots to their first Super Bowl victory, defeating the St. Louis Rams in Super Bowl XXXVI.

Over the years, Brady has become one of the most successful quarterbacks in NFL history. He has won six Super Bowl championships, four Super Bowl MVP awards, and has been selected to the Pro Bowl 14 times. Brady has also won numerous other awards and honors throughout his career, including the NFL Most Valuable Player award three times.

In 2020, Brady signed with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers after spending 20 seasons with the New England Patriots. In his first season with the Buccaneers, he led them to the Super Bowl, where they defeated the Kansas City Chiefs. Brady was named the Super Bowl MVP for the fifth time in his career.

Overall, Tom Brady’s career has been marked by excellence and success. He is widely considered to be one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time and has cemented his legacy as an NFL legend.
