Kim Newby of Waterloo, Iowa dies after battling with cancer: obituary

Waterloo, Iowa – Kim Newby, a beloved figure in Waterloo, Iowa, has passed away after a courageous battle with cancer. Denver Cyclone Wrestling conveyed the heartbreaking news of her passing through social media. Known as the Owner and Stylist at Goodlookin’ Salon, Kim’s untimely departure has left a profound impact on her community and those who knew her.

In a heartfelt social media post, it was confirmed that Kim Newby lost her battle with cancer, prompting an outpouring of condolences and support for her family, including Corbin, Cavin, Chris, and their extended circle, as they navigate this difficult loss.

The specific circumstances leading to Kim Newby’s passing, including the exact cause of her death, have not been disclosed at this time, leaving details surrounding her demise unknown. The community awaits further information, which will be shared with the public when available.

Denver Cyclone Wrestling posted on Facebook,

“We are saddened today by the news that Kim Newby lost her battle with cancer yesterday. We walk along side Corbin, Cavin, Chris, and their family and friends as they grieve this devastating loss.”

Goodlookin’ Salon, under Kim’s ownership, aimed to offer a serene and caring atmosphere. The salon’s philosophy revolved around a team-based approach, emphasizing a commitment to providing the highest level of care to clients. The salon’s diverse services, ranging from nail care to therapeutic massages and hair services, were dedicated to ensuring clients felt their best and looked forward to lasting relationships with them.

The Newby family will release details regarding Kim’s obituary and funeral arrangements, offering an opportunity for the community to pay respects and honor the legacy of a remarkable individual. Kim Newby’s impact on her clientele and the community will be cherished and remembered for years to come.
