Ian Ziering is an American actor, voice actor, producer, dancer, and writer who is best known for playing Steve Sanders/Ronnie Robertson in the teen drama series Beverly Hills, 90210, Vinnie (voice) in the animated sci-fi action series Biker Mice from Mars, Daniel Cassidy/Blue Devil in the superhero horror series Swamp Thing, and Fin Shepard in the Sharknado film series. Ian has also been cast in a variety of other shows including Melrose Place, Mighty Ducks, Hollywood Squares, Godzilla: The Series, Son of the Beach, Spider-Man, Drawn Together, Side Order of Life, CSI: NY, Hell’s Kitchen, Defiance, and The Thundermans.
