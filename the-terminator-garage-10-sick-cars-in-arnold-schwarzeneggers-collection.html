The Terminator Garage: 10 Sick Cars In Arnold Schwarzenegger's Collection

Arnold Schwarzenegger has truly lived a life of envy. It seems that every goal that he ever set his sights on was achieved. He is able to boast successes that the average person can only ever dream of. He has accomplished so many incredible things in life that had fans not actually witnessed his journey; it would be difficult to believe that he had actually done them all. He's a highly esteemed and incredibly successful actor, a former professional bodybuilder, a businessman, producer, and former politician who reigned supreme as the 38th Governor of California. With an astounding net worth of $400 million, Arnold Schwarzenegger can afford to splurge on anything his heart desires, and it seems he has an affinity for fine cars.

Excalibur: $89,000

One of the unique vehicles in Schwarzenegger's collection happens to be a classic vehicle that stands out among any others on the road. This vehicle is a re-creation of the Mercedes Benz SSK and oozes originality and class. It's a definite head-turner due to its vintage look and impressive styling. This vehicle is not a common choice for celebrities, and seeing Schwarzenegger behind the wheel makes it even more special. He dropped a cool $89,000 to be noticed in this ride.

Tesla Roadster: $100,000

Arnold Schwarzenegger had a Tesla before the brand was popularized. He was quick to the punch when he snagged one of the first Tesla Model S cars that ever went into production - the Roadster. This particular model was one of the first-ever fully electric cars off the line and was actually a huge flop for the Tesla brand. It lacked horsepower and was only able to push through to 248 hp upon its initial release. The current Roadster on the market has certainly seen huge improvements. Arnold spent $100,000 to be among the first to claim ownership of this environmentally friendly ride.

Hummer H1 Slant Back: $150,000

Arnold Schwarzenegger has an eclectic collection of vehicles, and among the most beasty of them, all is the Hummer H1 Slant Back that set him back $150,000. He opted for a light beige-colored model, which perfectly suits the brand and lends it the appearance of an aggressive vehicle that can handle any type of terrain. Schwarzenegger was often seen in this H1 and fully enjoyed his ability to drive this rugged vehicle.

Custom Audi R8: $180,000

Arnold is also attracted to the sleek styling and corner-hugging capabilities of the Audi R8. This speedy vehicle is among the top performers on the track and can reach impressive speeds with ease. Of course, Arnold took his Audi R8 ownership to the next level by fully customizing his car, inclusive of both aesthetic and performance-based enhancements. Fully designed to suit his every need, the cost of this Audi quickly skyrocketed to $180,000.

Bentley Continental GTC: $208,000

The incredible fleet of cars owned by Arnold Schwarzenegger includes some of the finest cars ever made, and his Bentley Continental GTC is among the most incredible machines in his possession. He fully customized this vehicle, too, and did a full-color change on it, turning it into a silver bullet made to take on the road. This car is powered by a V8 engine that combines both speed and styling, leading to a $208,000 price tag.

Porsche 911 Turbo Cabriolet: $250,000

Perhaps one of the finest, most recognized cars in his collection is the stunning Porsche 911 Turbo Cabriolet that Arnold Schwarzenegger is often spotted in. This incredible vehicle has an electrically powered wind deflector that extends or retracts in just 2 seconds with the simple press of a button. This gives the car increased speed capacity and aerodynamics, lending to its ability to hit its maximum speed of 205 mph with a 0-60 time of fewer than 2.7 seconds. Schwarzenegger didn't hesitate to invest $250,000 for this beauty.

Bentley Continental GT SuperSports Convertible: $320,000

Proving that Bentleys have truly captured his attention, Arnold has yet another one in his garage; this one is a powerful, sleek Continental GTC SuperSports convertible. This car boasts an impressive 6.0-liter W12 engine which cranks out 621 horsepower. Considered to be one of the most sought-after luxury cars on the market, it's not surprising that Schwarzenegger was willing to invest $320,000 to add this to his fleet.

Mercedes-Benz Unimog: $369,000

The Mercedes-Benz Unimog, owned by Arnold Schwarzenegger, is one of the most unique-looking vehicles in his garage. This unique and rare machine is actually not even considered to be street-legal in North America. The unique frame is built on top of a 1977 chassis. It's a short-cab that rides on top of 22-inch wheels that have enormous, oversized Michelin XZL 445/65 rubber. This was $369,000 well spent, considering how rare this head-turner really is.

Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG Roadster: $540,000

Switching gears to a vehicle that packs some punch when it comes to gorgeous styling and speedy performance, Schwarzenegger also happens to be the proud owner of a Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG Roadster. This convertible lacks gull wings but boasts a drop-top that adds to the sleek styling of the car. This incredibly fast supercar easily reaches 622 horsepower and oozes luxury at every turn. Schwarzenegger dropped a cool $540,000 to lay claims on this fine machine.

Bugatti Veyron: $1.7 Million

The most expensive, luxurious car in Arnold Schwarzenegger's fine car collection happens to have come with a $1.7 million price tag. His Bugatti Veyron is silver in color, and of course, he opted for the convertible model, enabling him to fully enjoy the Californian climate in style. This powerful beast features an 8.0 liter, 16 cylinder engine, which makes this Bugatti impressive on the road or track, and its sheer beauty in its styling and aesthetic appeal. The inside of this car is luxuriously designed, and it's easy to see why it was a must-have in Schwarzenegger's collection.
