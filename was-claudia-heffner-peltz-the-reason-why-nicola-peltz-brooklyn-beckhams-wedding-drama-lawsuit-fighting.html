How Did Claudia Heffner Peltz Affect Nicola Peltz And Brooklyn Beckham's Wedding?

Nicola wore a sophisticated wedding dress with gorgeous lace gloves. Hello! Nicola and Brooklyn worked with several wedding planners. But soon, fans learned that there was trouble in paradise.

Us Weekly reported that in December 2022, Nelson Peltz sued Arianna Grijalba and Nicole Braghin. He said they quit only nine days into planning and said that someone else should finish the job. Nelson also said that they owed him $159,000 since that was the deposit that he paid.

Based on Nicola Peltz's Instagram post, it does seem that she wanted to have a wedding that was a lot like her own mom and dad's. Nicola and Brooklyn got married at her family's Palm Beach, Florida house, which suggests that she wanted her family to be very involved.

People reported on the Nicola and Brooklyn wedding problems. The publication quoted the lawyer representing Nelson Peltz who said, "Peltz had presented them with the opportunity of a lifetime – to have their names associated with the wedding of two world-famous celebrities, which would undoubtedly propel PDE into wedding planning stardom."

PDE countersued and said, "It is believed that Nelson Peltz paid more than $100,000 plus travel expenses for Nicola's hair and makeup services for the wedding. Claudia mentioned to [one of the planners] that Nelson could not know the cost of the hair and makeup, or he would 'kill her, and be so mad.'"

Nicola continued to Cosmopolitan, "It’s more official as opposed to when you’re dating someone and you’re like, “Hey, that’s my boyfriend.” But in my head, I’m 14, so when I call myself a wife, I’m like, What? What’s going on? I’m literally a teen. I’m a preteen."
