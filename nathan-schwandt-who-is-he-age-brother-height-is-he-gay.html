Nathan Schwandt’s Age – Who Is He?

However, things change really fast for the young chap after he received a direct message from Jeffree Star in 2015. From an exchange on Instagram over time, Jeffree invited Nathan over to Los Angeles for their first hook up; the rest they say is history.
Nathan would eventually leave Michigan to go settle near his newfound love in Los Angeles.

He began working to expand Jeffree’s cosmetics company, Jeffree Star Cosmetics. After working in that capacity for a while, Nathan left work to go focus on tending their home and five dogs; Diva, Diamond, Daddy, Delicious, and Drama. They regard the animals as their “babies”.

So far, it has not been a fair ride for the young chap, seeing his conservative background and the stigma he continues to attract. Is Nathan Schwandt Truly Gay?

According to sources, Nathan’s family have been quite supportive of their sons’ relationship with Jeffree, which have given them quite some approval in the LGBT community. But many still disagree with his gay status, seeing his relationship history.

A lazy dig through his dating history shows that he only dated women in the past. This further lent weight to speculations that he chose to date Jeffree simply for the money. Nathan won’t let it slide. He explained that prior to dating Jeffree, he was still shy and timid about his gay orientation because he was locked up in a highly conservative setting back in Michigan; as such, he dated girls. However, he has been a lot prouder and bolder about his identity since he began dating Jeffree.
