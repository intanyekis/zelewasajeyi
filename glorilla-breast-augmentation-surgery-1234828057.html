GloRilla Addresses Undergoing Breast Enhancement Surgery

GloRilla is not shy about her choice to undergo plastic surgery. Speaking to her fans on social media last week, the Memphis native detailed the motivation behind her decision to go under the knife.

In the clip on the Instagram Live broadcast, shared by XXL across social media, the 24-year-old opens her jacket so viewers can see her newly enhanced bosom.

“I’m on my bullsh*t, I just bought some new titties,” revealed the Grammy-nominated performer. “Y’all know I ain’t with that overboard sh*t, these a lil’ fill-in. Just filled them in. They ain’t nothing too outrageous. Just something for my man to grip on.”

Earlier this year, the “Lick Or Sum” rapper shared her latest release Anyways, Life’s Great Deluxe in April after dropping the original EP in November 2022. The updated 13-track effort features Lil Durk, Cardi B, Trina, Gloss Up, NikiPooh, and Hitkidd.

“You see [with] the EP, three, four of the songs I wrote before I got into the industry—that was me being me fresh into the industry,” she explained to ELLE of the project earlier this summer.

GloRilla recently wrapped the Gangsta Art tour, led by her CMG boss Yo Gotti, and featuring labelmates Big Boogie, Blac Youngsta, Moneybagg Yo, EST Gee, Lil Poppa, and Mozzy. Kicking off last month in Texas, the journey wrapped last week in North Carolina.
