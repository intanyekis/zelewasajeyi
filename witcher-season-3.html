First Look At Henry Cavill's Final Season In The Teaser Trailer

In the season three first-look teaser trailer for Netflix’s The Witcher, Henry Cavill is returning for his last season. The video, which was made public along with the announcement that the fantasy drama will return this summer in two parts, with the tagline “Everything Changes.”

In addition, Netflix provided the following summary of season three, which is based on the book series and video game adaptations by author Andrzej Sapkowski: Geralt sends Ciri (Freya Allan) into hiding while kings, mages, and monsters of the Continent compete to capture her, determined to safeguard his recently reunited family against those who threaten to destroy them.

In order to learn more about Ciri’s latent abilities, Yennefer (Anya Chalotra), who has been entrusted with her magical training, directs them to the safe haven of Aretuza. However, when they arrive, they find themselves in the middle of a war between political intrigue, dark magic, and betrayal. They have to fight back and risk everything if they don’t want to lose each other forever.
