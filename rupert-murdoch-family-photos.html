Rupert Murdoch &amp; Family: Photos Hollywood Life

Rupert Murdoch is one of the most powerful men in the world, said to be worth over $17billion, according to ‘Forbes’. With that big of a bank account, he has some interesting personal ties. Rupert’s eldest is Prudence Murdoch, from his first marriage to Australian model Patricia Booker, from 1956 to 1966.

His next three children are from his second marriage to Anna Murdoch Mann. Sons Lachlan and James helped run the family company Newscorp, which owns Fox News and other brands, for many years. James resigned in 2020, leaving his younger brother to lead. Rupert also has girls Grace and Chloe with ex Wendy Deng, married from 1999 to 2013.

Rupert was married to model Jerry Hall from 2016 until news of their split in 2022. The bombshell news was reported by ‘The New York Times’.

Here, Lachlan Murdoch, Rupert Murdoch and James Murdoch pose during his wedding to Jerry Hall at London’s St. Bride’s Church on Fleet Street on Mar. 5, 2016. They looked so happy to be together.
